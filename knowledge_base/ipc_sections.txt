Relevant Legal References for Women's Safety (India)

Section 354 IPC:
Assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty.

Section 509 IPC:
Word, gesture or act intended to insult the modesty of a woman.

Police must respond immediately if physical assault is suspected.

Section 354A IPC:
Sexual harassment including unwelcome physical contact or advances.

Section 354D IPC:
Stalking, including monitoring or following a woman repeatedly.

Authorities must prioritize response when physical harassment or stalking behavior is suspected.
