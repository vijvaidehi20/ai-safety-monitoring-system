Example Safety Scenarios

Scenario 1:
A woman suddenly falls in a poorly lit street at 11:30 PM while two individuals are standing nearby.
Risk Level: HIGH
Reason: Night-time environment, low lighting, and sudden fall increase potential safety risk.

Scenario 2:
A woman is walking normally in a well-lit public area with several people nearby.
Risk Level: LOW
Reason: Normal behavior patterns and safe environmental conditions.

Scenario 3:
A person repeatedly gestures for help while standing alone in a dark area.
Risk Level: HIGH
Reason: Distress gestures combined with low visibility indicate potential danger.

Scenario 4:
A group of three individuals surrounds a single person and aggressive gestures are detected.
Risk Level: CRITICAL
Reason: Crowd dynamics and aggressive behavior indicate possible harassment or assault.

Scenario 5:
A person briefly stumbles but quickly stands up and continues walking normally.
Risk Level: LOW
Reason: Temporary loss of balance without additional suspicious activity.


Example Safety Scenarios for AI Monitoring

LOW Risk Scenario:
A person briefly stumbles while walking but immediately regains balance and continues normally. The area is well lit and other people are nearby behaving normally.

MEDIUM Risk Scenario:
A person appears confused or repeatedly gestures for help in a moderately crowded area but no aggressive behavior is detected.

HIGH Risk Scenario:
A woman suddenly falls in a poorly lit street at night while two individuals are standing nearby. The situation requires immediate monitoring and possible escalation.

CRITICAL Risk Scenario:
A group of individuals surrounds a single person while aggressive gestures are detected in a dark area at night. This situation may indicate harassment or assault and requires immediate intervention.
