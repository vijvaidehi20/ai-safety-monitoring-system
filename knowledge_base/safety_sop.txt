Women Safety Monitoring Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

1. If a woman is detected falling suddenly in a public place, the system must first assess whether it is an accidental fall or a potential assault.

2. If the incident occurs during night hours (8 PM – 5 AM), risk level increases.

3. If the area is poorly lit, the risk score increases.

4. If more than one unidentified person is standing close to the victim within 2 meters, escalate to medium or high risk.

5. For high-risk events, notify nearby authorities immediately and activate public announcement system.

6. For medium-risk events, issue warning announcement before escalation.
