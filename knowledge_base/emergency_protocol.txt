Emergency Response Protocol

HIGH Risk Events
- Immediately notify nearby authorities.
- Send captured frame evidence.
- Continue monitoring the location for additional suspicious activity.

CRITICAL Risk Events
- Trigger emergency alert immediately.
- Notify law enforcement and nearby security personnel.
- Broadcast public safety warning if available.

MEDIUM Risk Events
- Monitor the situation for 30 seconds.
- If suspicious behavior continues or escalates, upgrade the risk level.

LOW Risk Events
- Continue passive monitoring without alerting authorities.
